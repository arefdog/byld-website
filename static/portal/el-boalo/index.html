Madrid Sierra · El Boalo · Sector 1B · Agency brief

A serviced mountain estate for Madrid, forty-five minutes from the city.

A private collection of villas and residences on a seven-hectare estate in the Sierra de Guadarrama, set within a further twenty hectares of protected countryside, all meadow, dehesa and granite, with a wellness hotel, spa and equestrian centre at its heart. This is what we are building, who it is for, and what it sells for.

Who it is for

The buyer is the affluent madrileño who wants nature without leaving the city behind, and the international buyer who wants a capital on the doorstep and countryside living beyond it. Madrid is one of Europe's largest and wealthiest metropolitan markets, and it is forty-five minutes away.

Door to door in about forty-five minutes: from Madrid Centro, up the A-6 or M-607, to the foot of the sierra.

Two audiences buy here. Primary and second-home buyers from greater Madrid, who want space, air and landscape inside a commute. And guests, who come first for a night at the hotel and buy afterwards. The hotel is the shop window: the sierra has no five-star address, and Madrid's luxury hotels are all in the city.

We enter at the floor of the category. The band runs from around €4,375/m² up through the €6–15k Mediterranean, Tuscan and lake estates to €16–25k in the high Alps and the Aman tier. Our pricing sits at the entry, with the whole category above us.

No peer combines what we have. A metropolitan market of 7.1 million, larger than Lisbon or Athens, forty-five minutes away, and a genuine mountain landscape. The city-adjacent peers are urban seafront, not countryside. The great rural estates sit two to three hours from Rome or Athens. We have both.

Four seasons, so demand runs all year. At 920 m the estate is a cool-air escape from Madrid's summers, has snow within reach in winter, and temperate spring and autumn. The Mediterranean peers are summer-led and the Alpine peers winter-led. We are open, and sought after, in every month.

The brand premium is there to take. Branded residences command roughly +32% in resort markets. An operator affiliation on this estate captures that uplift directly.

06

The estate can grow

The land around the sector is available to us, and we intend to take it.

Adjacent parcels can be brought into the estate to extend its private landscape well beyond the sector boundary. That land carries the grounds, the trail network, the equestrian paddocks and the farm, and it secures the horizon in perpetuity: nothing is ever built between these homes and the sierra.

For a buyer, that is the difference between a view and an owned view. It widens the estate from seven hectares to a landholding measured in tens, and it gives the scheme room to add further phases of homes and hospitality as demand proves out.

07

A call to Madrid agents

Present the estate to your buyers and tell us what comes back. We are looking for hard signal, not opinion, and for the partners who will bring it to market with us.

  • Appetite at the price points.

    Open-market villas from ~€850,000 and premium, branded villas from ~€1.5M. Where does interest concentrate, where does it break, and what are buyers looking for?

  • The two buyer routes.

    Greater-Madrid primary and second-home buyers, and international buyers who want a capital within an hour. Which converts faster?

  • Product and size.

    Preferred villa sizes, plot sizes and typologies, so the mix is shaped by demand.

  • The pull of the serviced offer.

    How much of the premium is carried by the hotel, spa, equestrian centre and the guaranteed open horizon? And which operator would draw the buyer: a culinary destination, a wellness house, a sporting estate? We are actively seeking a partner to curate the programme.

  • Reservation intent.

    Expressions of interest and reservation appetite off-plan, with the deposit level buyers will commit at.
