The site and the binding planning

The plot is Sector 1B of the El Boalo NNSS. The official ordenación ficha, published in the BOCM, gives the binding parameters. It is developable residential land, obtained by agreement with the owner, not protected rústico.

The parameters describe an exclusive, low-density villa estate: eleven homes per hectare, two storeys, an average home near 226 m², and half the land ceded as public roads and green space, run by a private conservation entity. The planning itself prescribes the premium, landscape-led product, and the low delivered density is the strongest answer to local opposition to urban expansion.

03

Recommended use

The planning caps units and the market rewards quality, so value comes from price per home, not more homes. The answer is a premium villa estate with a small wellness hotel as its amenity and brand.

Residential is the engine. Up to 78 low-density homes, with the VPP obligation (30% of residential edificability under Ley 9/2001) held at the floor and delivered as a compact block near the village edge, and the free-market homes placed as villas on the south-facing contours.

A wellness hotel is the amenity, not the anchor. A small hotel (roughly 50 to 70 keys) exploits the five-star gap and, more importantly, turns the homes into serviced, branded residences that command a premium and sell faster to the greater-Madrid buyer. It is sized to a seasonal proximity market, not underwritten as a large resort.

The setting is bought cheaply. Adjacent rústico land, available at a fraction of buildable value, can hold the grounds, trails and views and protect the horizon permanently, moving the open-space cost off expensive sector land.

06

What to verify next

  • PGOU redraft and local opposition.

    The 1996 NNSS is being replaced; confirm Sector 1B survives the new plan. The low-density, half-green scheme is the best political defence.

  • Vía pecuaria deslinde.

    The ficha requires authorisation from Vías Pecuarias and Comisaría de Aguas; secure the deslinde early, as it shapes the developable area and the mandated Ronda Este connection.

  • Cession and urbanization load.

    Only 50% of the land is privately appropriable, plus a 10% aprovechamiento cession and full urbanization within 8 years. Model it fully before pricing the land.

  • Plan Parcial unit mix.

    Resolve the 30% VPP against the 78-home cap and large average home size, with a compact VPP block and large free-market villas.
