What this shows

A physically accurate sun for the selected location, lit by a physically-based sky. The round roof casts a real shadow onto the deck and the central glass core. Choose a location, drag Day, Time and Overhang, switch the glazing, or press Play – the heat table below recomputes live.

  • The overhang is the main solar control. Drag it wider and the glass stays shaded down to lower sun angles; pull it in and more direct beam reaches the core. Watch the annual figure respond.
  • Only low sunrise/sunset light reaches under the eaves. Set Time to ~6:00 or ~19:00 to see the rays rake across the deck and into the core.
  • The winter trade-off: a deep overhang blocks summer heat beautifully – but also shades the low December sun you might want to welcome in. The slider lets you find the balance.
